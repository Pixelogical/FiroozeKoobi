The export process

In the current century, distribution of value and valuation of local products for global markets are achieved through export efforts in a much more focused manner compared to the past. Nowadays, a lot of companies around the world are capable of entering global markets; however, few have made it their mission to transfer values and culture in the free global trade. In this regard, Artco tries to provide international traders and firms with clear and practical information about issues such as export values of the products, export infrastructures, drafting of the contracts and transportation of the products. In the first place, this is done with the aim of observing the ethical principles of exports and in the second place, with the goal of supplying fundamental information to international traders and businesses to satisfy basic information needs.

Artco in exports

Artco was established in 2012 with a focus on creating handcrafted, deluxe products. In the first step, Artco attempted to satisfy the market demand in Iran by identifying and hiring the best Iranian master craftsmen in the field of turquoise inlay as well as training 30 talented recruits. From 2012 to 2015, Artco expanded its operations by going from 1 central workshop to 8 workshops around Iran and by increasing the number of its employed master craftsmen to 130, thereby becoming one of the main producers in the field. The expansion enabled Artco to act according to its export prospects and satisfy the demands of global markets as well as those of the domestic market. Currently, Artco manufactures 1400 products per month with help from top experts in the field of market research, who have contributed to the preparation of infrastructure for manufacturing of products for global markets. It has successfully managed to carry out all export operations relevant to turquoise inlay products, from manufacturing to secure transportation to Western Asia and Eastern Europe. In addition, the International Manufacturing unit in Artco cooperates with leading experts in the fields of branding and marketing to maintain a dynamic process of identifying the culture and nature of demands in the target countries. In doing so, the company seeks to bring about a practical distinction in the production method of international products and their packaging, commensurate with each target country and its traders.
