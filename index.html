Low weight, elegance and durability are three key factors in packaging of exported goods. Taking into account the cultures of the target countries, transportation issues and the features of every product, The Package Design and Manufacturing Unit in Artco Company has designed exclusive, customized packages and makes use of functional packaging in exports.
On-time delivery of products according to the delivery schedule per contract, as well as observing global standards of transportation in terms of the number and the unharmed state of delivered goods are the cornerstones of ensuring the satisfaction of Artco`s international customers.
Inherent beauty of handicrafts and manufacturing of exclusive packages according to type of the product and the cultural context of target countries are important factors contributing to the creation of export value and the selling of this value and art in the global free market. Artco takes pride in the fact that, in an attempt to establish mutual relations with international traders and exchanging information with them, it has achieved practical and profound levels of behavior analysis and shopping behavior analysis with respect to the target market in each of the countries, and has covered all the demands of the international markets.
